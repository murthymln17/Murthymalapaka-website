About Murthy

Executive technology strategist. AI transformation leader. Partner to enterprises rethinking how they operate.

Murthy Malapaka works at the intersection of technology strategy and enterprise leadership — helping organizations move past AI experimentation into operating models that actually change how decisions get made.

His career began in hands-on technology leadership before moving into strategic advisory, giving him a rare combination of technical fluency and executive judgment. That background shapes how he works today: not as a technologist selling a roadmap, but as a partner to leadership teams who need to make hard operating decisions under real constraints.

Today, his focus is squarely on AI-led transformation — helping CEOs, CIOs, and boards redesign decision rights, talent models, and governance so that AI capability translates into durable business value, rather than another wave of pilots that never scale.

He works directly with senior leadership teams, in the room where the hard trade-offs get made, rather than at arm's length through slide decks.
