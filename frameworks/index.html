Frameworks

Practical models for leading AI transformation and redesigning how the enterprise operates.

The AI Operating Model Canvas

A single-page model for aligning strategy, decision rights, talent, and technology before committing capital to AI transformation.

01

Diagnose

Map how decisions, data, and talent currently flow through the organization.

02

Design

Define the target operating model across people, process, and technology.

03

Sequence

Prioritize initiatives by business value and organizational readiness.

04

Govern

Establish the decision rights and metrics that sustain the change.

The Transformation Value Loop

A closed-loop approach that ties every AI initiative to a measurable business outcome within two quarters, not two years.

01

Commit

Define the outcome and the accountable owner before funding the initiative.

02

Deliver

Ship in production increments — not indefinite pilots.

03

Reinforce

Feed results back into strategy and resourcing decisions.

The Human + AI Operating Model

A framework for redesigning roles and decision rights so people and AI systems complement each other rather than compete.

01

Separate

Distinguish judgment work from execution work.

02

Redesign

Rebuild roles around the judgment humans uniquely provide.

03

Integrate

Embed AI into workflows with clear accountability and oversight.
